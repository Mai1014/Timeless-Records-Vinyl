Reflections in Blue

Velvet Reverie's latest album is an evocative journey through introspection and hope.

With a blend of dream pop and indie rock, the album invites listeners into a soundscape of lush melodies and poignant lyrics.

Tracklist

  1. Echoes of the Mind - A gentle instrumental opener setting the mood.

  2. The Path Less Played - A soulful exploration of new beginnings.
  3. Cycles and Seasons - An introspective journey of self-discovery.
  4. Midnight Meditations - A dreamy ballad about fleeting moments.
  5. The Ripple Effect - A vibrant, uplifting track to inspire hope.
  6. Notes of Intention - A powerful conclusion that ties the album together.
  7. Harmony of Now - A serene reflection on the present moment.
  8. The Jazz of Being - A vibrant celebration of life.
  9. Fragile Threads - A poignant exploration of loss and love.
  10. Infinite Horizons - An uplifting anthem of hope and dreams.
