About the Artist

Welcome to the world of Velvet Reverie, a band that blends dreamy soundscapes with soulful melodies. Formed in 2015, Velvet Reverie has captivated audiences worldwide with their unique approach to storytelling through music.

Their latest album, "Reflections in Blue", takes listeners on a journey through themes of introspection, hope, and renewal. Let the music guide you to a place of wonder.
